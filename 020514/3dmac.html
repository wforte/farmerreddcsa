WELCOME, all friends and visitors to The Farm website. Village of OWEGO, NY had been voted, “The coolest small town in America 2009”, by Budget Travel Magazine readers and sits on the north banks of the mighty Susquehanna River. The Farm is situated in the Southern Tier of NY State, an area covering RT17 (now RT86) west from the Catskill Mountains and south along the northern PA border.

The Farm uses the magical processes of nature to produce organic fruits and vegetables. Farmer Redd have owned and managed The Farm the past 2 years, 2013 being a banner year for the CSA growers. CSA is an acronym for “Community Supported Agriculture”, a concept of consumers buying local, seasonal products directly from the farmer. It is becoming more popular in NY State and all across the country as younger consumers want organic fruits and vegetables for themselves and their children. As demand has increased, more small farmers, like myself, are using their land to provide for their communities.

There is another side of today’s small farmer: Self-reliance. It is also known as homesteading. It is a lifestyle that is built around self-sufficiency and utilizing the resources that the planet and nature provides. It’s a feeling of being in control of aspects of your life that otherwise are not available to most people worldwide. Feel free to visit all the topics in and about The Farm website.
